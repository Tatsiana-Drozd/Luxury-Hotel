Room Overview

Experience the ultimate family getaway in our spacious suite, offering separate sleeping areas and a shared living space for quality time together.

Bed & Bath

  • Bedroom 1: One king‐size bed (180 cm) with premium mattress
  • Bedroom 2: Two single beds (90 cm each) OR one bunk bed

Bathroom: Two bathrooms (one en‐suite, one guest bathroom) – no more morning queues! A spacious marble bathroom equipped with a rainfall shower and a separate deep‐soaking bathtub. Complimentary organic toiletries, plush bathrobes, and slippers are provided.

Amenities & Technology

  • High‐Speed Wi‐Fi: Free unlimited internet access for up to 5 devices.
  • Entertainment: A 55‐inch 4K Smart TV with international satellite channels and Chromecast streaming.
  • Climate Control: Individual air conditioning and heating system with a digital thermostat.
  • Work Desk: An ergonomic workspace with multiple power outlets (universal sockets) and a reading lamp.

In‐Room Features

  • Mini‐bar with selected soft drinks and snacks (charges apply)
  • Nespresso coffee machine with complimentary capsules
  • Electric kettle with a selection of premium teas
  • Digital safe (fits up to a 15‐inch laptop)
  • Iron and ironing board upon request
  • Blackout curtains for undisturbed sleep
